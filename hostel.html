HOSTEL ROOMS

A hostel campus should be a place where students can have the best possible conditions for studying and adequate rest. As such due consideration must be accorded to other residents at all times. Noise level must be kept low to allow others’ the opportunity to study or sleep in comfort.

In case of any unacceptable behaviour by the room-mate, the other room-mate must report to authorities

Residents are responsible for keeping their rooms and the common areas in the hostel such as visitor’s area, bathrooms, stair case, and common room etc. clean and tidy at all times.

Cooking, making tea etc. is not allowed in the hostel.

The hostel management reserves the right to revise the rules and regulations from time to time and will keep the resident informed of any changes in the form of notices on the hostel notice boards.
